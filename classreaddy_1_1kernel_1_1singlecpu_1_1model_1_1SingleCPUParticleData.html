void readdy::kernel::singlecpu::model::SingleCPUParticleData::deactivateMarked ( )

This method is the counterpart to markForDeactivation. The particles that were marked are now deactivated, i.e., for each marked particle:

  • If it is at the very end of the particle list, the counters are updated.
  • If not, the particle is swapped with the last active particle, so that again, all deactivated particles reside at the end of the internal data structure.
